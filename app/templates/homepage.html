Welcome to InteractiveGW!

Dive into the world of gravitational waves generated by a binary black hole merger!

Generate real-time gravitational wave signals and download videos!

InteractiveGW uses state of the art SXS Surrogate model NRSur7dq4, signals so its almost as accurate as numerical relativity generated signals. Learn more...

Interactive GW Strain


Make interactive plots for gravitational wave strain \( {\color{yellowgreen}h(t)} \),

\( {\color{yellowgreen}{h(t, \iota, \phi_0)}} {\color{skyblue} \, = \sum_{l=2}^{\infty} \sum_{m=-l}^{l} h_{lm}(t) \, Y_{lm}(\iota, \phi_0)} \)

Vary the parameters of binary black holes, inclination, azimuthal angle to see how the strain depends on these paramters.

Interactive GW \(h_{lm} \) modes


Make interactive plots for gravitational wave \({\color{yellowgreen}h_{lm}} \) modes when expanded as

\( {\color{skyblue} h(t, \iota, \phi_0) \, = \sum_{l=2}^{\infty} \sum_{m=-l}^{l} {\color{yellowgreen}h_{lm}(t)} \, Y_{lm}(\iota, \phi_0)} \)

Vary the parameters of binary black holes and spherical harmonic modes to see how the strain depends on these paramters.
